About Us

Our love affair with cakes began back in 2002 when we opened our first Cakes & Bakes branch in San Antonio, Texas, USA. Ever since opening our first store to the public in 2002 Baked-Bites-By-Bel has been going from strength to strength. At Baked-Bites-By-Bel we believe in providing only the freshest and most delicious cakes to our customers. Our success is based on our reputation for quality and freshness, which we've maintained since opening our first store. Whether you're looking for a stunning fresh cream, buttercream, or fondant whole cake, cupcakes, or something savory, we have something perfect for you. Taste and see!. See you soon. Claire has always loved designing and decorating cakes, having joined the company in 2009 she has been working hard to develop our much loved “house style”. As creative director of the company Claire designs all of the cakes produced from the client’s initial idea. Claire’s background was studying fine art in Brighton and she loves the creative process of designing a cake. Her cakes begin life in her sketchbooks which are full of colour swatches, patterns, drawings and inspirations. Her love of photography goes hand-in hand with her cakes and she can often been seen snapping away on her camera! It is Claire’s aim to ensure that the company remains at the cutting edge of cake design in Scotland.
