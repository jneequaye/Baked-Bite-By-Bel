Delivery Information:

Our handmade wedding cakes are fragile and require extra care during transport, which is why we recommend opting for driver delivery in our refrigerated vans, so they are in perfect condition when they arrive at your door.

If opting for store collection, we strongly advise your cake is not left out of the fridge during transit to your destination for more than 30 minutes.
